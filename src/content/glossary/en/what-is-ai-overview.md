## What Is AI Overview: How Google Changed Its Search Results

In May 2024, Google changed its search results page. Above the list of links, a box appeared with an answer generated directly by AI. This is AI Overview.

When a user searches, the AI reads dozens of web pages and produces a summarized, synthesized answer on the spot. Users no longer need to click through to a link. That is why being included inside the AI Overview has become more important than ranking first in the traditional search results.

## How Google AI Overview Selects Its Sources

Google AI Overview operates on top of the existing Google search index. Pages with strong SEO are more likely to be selected as AI Overview sources, but SEO rankings and AI Overview citations do not always align.

The characteristics of content that AI Overview favors are clear. Clear sentences that answer the question directly, trustworthy sources and data, content with strong E-E-A-T signals, and pages with structured data applied all have a higher likelihood of being cited.

## Creating Content That Gets Selected as an AI Overview Source

Place a clear definition box at the top of the page — AI Overview references the first paragraph of your content the most. Position the core answer to the question in one or two sentences directly below the H1.

Use question-style H2 and H3 headings — AI prioritizes the heading structure that best matches the user's query. Natural-language headings such as "What is AI Overview?" or "How do you optimize for AI Overview?" are effective.

Cite specific data and sources — AI Overview favors verifiable information. Including statistics, research findings, and official announcements in your body copy increases the likelihood of being cited.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. If my site appears as a source in AI Overview, does it generate traffic?

Some users do click the source links shown as footnotes, so a portion of traffic is generated. However, the click-through rate is lower than that of traditional search results. The value lies more in building brand trust than in driving traffic.

Q. Does AI Overview reduce my site's traffic?

Since AI Overview was introduced, some sites have seen a decline in clicks. However, clicks still occur for complex topics and purchase-decision queries. What matters is redesigning your content strategy for the AI Overview era.

Q. What do I do if AI Overview shows incorrect information about my brand?

Google currently does not provide an official channel to directly edit AI Overview results. Revising your original content and updating it with accurate information can indirectly influence the outcome.
