## How Often Does Zero-Click Search Happen

Far more often than you might think.

As of 2024, research indicates that more than roughly 60% of Google searches end without a click. The zero-click rate is especially high on mobile, and since AI Overviews began rolling out at scale, this figure has continued to climb.

In other words, situations where you invest heavily in SEO and improve your search rankings yet generate no clicks are becoming more common.

## Why Zero-Click Search Happens: Which Elements Block the Click

## Search Result Elements That Trigger Zero-Click

AI Overview — Google's AI generates the answer directly and displays it at the very top. Users get the information they need from this answer and end their search.

Featured Snippet — For queries with clear-cut answers such as definitions, how-tos, and lists, it presents the answer directly in a box.

Knowledge Panel — Displays summary information about companies, people, and places as a card on the right-hand side.

People Also Ask — Clicking a related question expands the answer right there. There's no need to navigate to another site.

Weather, Exchange Rates, Calculator — Instant-answer widgets display the result directly.

## Strategies Brands Can Adopt in the Zero-Click Era

## 3 Ways to Get Brand Exposure Without Clicks

Approach 1: Make getting into the AI answer itself the goal.

Even without a click, when your brand name is mentioned in an AI Overview or Featured Snippet, awareness builds up. If a user searches "recommended telemedicine apps" and the AI mentions Dr. Now, brand exposure occurs even without a click. GEO and AEO are what make this strategy a reality.

Approach 2: Focus on keywords that don't end in zero-click.

Not every keyword ends in a zero-click. Queries that demand in-depth information, comparisons, purchase decisions, or long-form content still drive clicks. Your keyword strategy should deliberately increase this type.

Approach 3: Connect with channels beyond search.

A multi-channel strategy that ties in LinkedIn, email, and community channels—so that users who ended their search with zero clicks encounter your brand again afterward—has become essential.

## The Connection Between Zero-Click Search and SEO, GEO, AEO

## An Integrated SEO, GEO, AEO Approach That Turns Zero-Click to Your Advantage

Paradoxically, appearing in the very Featured Snippets and AI Overviews that trigger zero-click searches has itself become a way to build brand equity. It's a structure where you "lose the click but gain the exposure."

From this perspective, AEO is the strategy that responds most directly to the zero-click environment, because getting into AI Overviews and Featured Snippets is precisely the goal of AEO.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. As zero-click search grows, does SEO become meaningless?

No. Even as zero-click grows, clicks still occur for brand searches, purchase-intent queries, and in-depth information queries. The purpose of SEO has shifted—its importance hasn't disappeared.

Q. Should I avoid keywords with high zero-click rates?

Rather than avoiding them, a better strategy is to optimize your content so your brand gets into the zero-click zones (AI Overviews, Featured Snippets). The exposure itself creates brand value.

Q. How do I measure the zero-click rate?

You can check the ratio of clicks to impressions (CTR) in Google Search Console. The lower the CTR for a keyword, the stronger the signal that zero-click is occurring frequently.
