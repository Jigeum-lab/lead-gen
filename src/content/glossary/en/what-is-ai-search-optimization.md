## Why AI Search Optimization Has Become Necessary

Starting in 2025, search behavior split in two directions.

On one side are users who still type keywords into Google and click links. On the other side, the number of users who ask ChatGPT questions in natural language and get answers on the spot is growing rapidly.

As the second group grows, you end up with users you simply cannot reach through traditional SEO alone. AI Search Optimization is the strategy for getting your brand in front of that second group.

## The Scope of AI Search Optimization: Which Platforms You Need to Cover

## Global AI Search Platforms

ChatGPT — More than 500 million monthly active users. Content is cited frequently in brand and product recommendation queries.

Perplexity — An AI search engine that explicitly cites its sources. It prioritizes citing specialized, in-depth content.

Google AI Overview — The generative AI answer displayed at the very top of Google search results. Both SEO and GEO influence it at the same time.

Microsoft Copilot — Bing-based AI search. It has a high share of enterprise users.

## AI Search Platforms in the Korean Market

Naver AI Briefing — Naver's AI-powered search interface. In the Korean market, its influence can be greater than that of Google AI Overview.

## The Core Strategic Elements of AI Search Optimization

## Four Ways to Get Your Brand Surfaced in AI Search

1. Strengthen content authority (E-E-A-T) — AI favors trustworthy sources. Author information, professional credentials, and content grounded in real-world experience increase the likelihood of being cited.

2. Apply structured data (Schema Markup) — Apply schema in JSON-LD format so that AI crawlers accurately understand your content's structure.

3. Natural-language, question-driven content — Structure your content around the kinds of sentences users would actually ask an AI. H2 and H3 structures that begin with "how," "why," and "what" are effective.

4. Allow AI crawler access — In your robots.txt, you need to allow access for major AI crawlers such as GPTBot, ClaudeBot, and PerplexityBot. If they are blocked, no content—no matter how good—will be learned by the AI.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Are AI Search Optimization and SEO completely different things?

They share a common foundation. Content quality, technical stability, and authority matter for both SEO and AI Search Optimization. On top of that, however, AI search additionally requires content structure, natural-language suitability, and crawler accessibility.

Q. Where can I verify the results of AI Search Optimization?

The most direct method is to check AI referral traffic coming from sources such as chatgpt.com and perplexity.ai in the Source/Medium report in Google Analytics.

Q. Can AI Search Optimization be outsourced?

Yes. The key, though, is to choose a partner who approaches GEO and AEO in an integrated way rather than treating them as separate from SEO.
