## LeadGenLab walks a single path: AI optimization

LeadGenLab began as a team of specialists in lead generation — the marketing craft of growing prospects and revenue. Whether B2B or B2C, the essence of marketing has always been one question: "How do we get chosen more often?"

For years, the default answer was to grow traffic. But how customers are acquired is changing. Consumers no longer compare search results themselves. AI compares, condenses, and recommends on their behalf.

That is why LeadGenLab focuses not on SEO or ad agency work, but on **AI Visibility Optimization (AVO)**. The future of lead generation is designing the structure that AI recommends.

## From the traffic era to the era of AI selection probability

In the past, the journey was "keyword search → click → landing page → conversion," and the search box was the first touchpoint with the customer. Today it flows as "AI question → brand included in the AI answer → AI recommendation → purchase." The AI answer has become the first touchpoint.

We do not simply optimize click-through rates. We optimize the probability that AI selects your brand, and the inflow and conversion that come through AI.
